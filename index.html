ABOUT US

Information About Us

Welcome to Vetson Animal Healthcare, your trusted partner in veterinary care. Established in 23 August 2022, we are dedicated to providing innovative and high-quality medicines for pets. Our mission is to enhance the health and well-being of animals through advanced research and development. At Vetson, we believe in the power of science and compassion, working closely with veterinarians worldwide to ensure the best outcomes for pets. Join us in our commitment to making a difference in the lives of animals everywhere.

Company History

Vetson Animal Health Care Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 23 August 2022. It is classified as Non-government company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, ROC Patna. Its authorized share capital is Rs. 1,000,000 and its paid up capital is Rs. 100,000. It is inolved in Other wholesale [Includes specialized wholesale not covered in any one of the previous categories and wholesale in a variety of goods without any particular specialization.] Vetson Animal Health Care Private Limited's Annual General Meeting (AGM) was last held on N/A and as per records from Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), its balance sheet was last filed on 31 March 2023.
